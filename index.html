① Pluggable Camp — "The USB Drive Translators"① 外接派——「USB隨身碟翻譯官」

LPO Linear Pluggable Optics線性驅動光學

Think of a translator who boldly removes all grammar-checking equipment (DSP), letting the speaker's raw message pass through directly. This saves ~50% power and adds near-zero delay — but if the "speaker" (signal) mumbles, there's no safety net. Best for short, clean links where signal quality is already excellent. The switch chip on the other end handles any error correction itself. 想像一位翻譯官大膽地移除所有文法校正設備（DSP），讓說話者的原始訊息直接通過。省電約50%且幾乎零延遲——但如果「說話者」（訊號）含糊不清，就沒有安全網。最適合訊號品質已經很好的短距離乾淨鏈路。另一端的交換器晶片自行處理糾錯。
InnolightEoptolinkCOHRLITE

LRO Linear Receive Optics線性接收光學

The pragmatic translator — and the real 2026 production mainstream. LRO only removes the grammar-checker on the listening side (receiver DSP), while keeping it on the speaking side (transmitter). This saves 30-40% power with much better compatibility than full LPO. Already adopted at scale by Google and Meta. Think of it as: "I'll speak carefully, but I trust you to listen without a filter." 務實翻譯官——2026年真正的量產主力。LRO只移除聆聽端（接收端DSP）的文法校正器，保留說話端（發送端）的糾錯能力。省電30-40%，互通性遠優於完整LPO。已被Google與Meta大規模採用。可以理解為：「我會謹慎說話，但相信你不需要濾波器就能聽懂。」
InnolightEoptolinkCOHRLITEFN

XPO eXtra-dense Pluggable Optics極致可插拔光學

When the front panel can't fit any more standard modules, Arista's answer is: build a giant USB drive with built-in water cooling. XPO is 2.7× the size of a standard OSFP module, but packs 12.8Tbps per module — the bandwidth of 8 traditional modules in one. It has an integrated liquid cold plate that handles 400W+ of heat with 40-45°C warm water, and runs on a 50V DC bus to ease the motherboard's electrical burden. The result: a 128,000-GPU AI factory needs only 352 switch racks instead of 1,408. Crucially, it preserves hot-swap repairability — pull it out like a USB, replace, done. 當前面板再也塞不下更多標準模組時，Arista的答案是：做一個帶內建水冷的超級大USB隨身碟。XPO的體積是標準OSFP模組的2.7倍，但單模組達12.8Tbps——一個模組等於8個傳統模組的頻寬。內建液冷散熱板，以40-45°C溫水輕鬆帶走400W+熱量，並採用50V直流供電減輕主機板電流負擔。結果：12.8萬顆GPU的AI工廠只需352個交換器機架而非1,408個。關鍵是保留了熱插拔可維修性——像拔USB一樣拉出來更換，完成。
ANETLightmatterEoptolinkTeraHop

② Internal Camp — "Moving the Translator Closer to the Boss"② 內建派——「把翻譯官搬到老闆身邊」

NPO Near-Packaged Optics近封裝光學

The brilliant compromise. Traditional pluggable modules are like a translator standing at the front door; CPO welds the translator into the boss's brain. NPO takes the middle path: it moves the translator to a desk right next to the boss (millimeters from the chip), but in a socket, not soldered. This solves all four CPO problems at once: ❶ If it breaks, just unplug — no scrapping the entire board. ❷ It avoids the chip's thermal hell zone. ❸ It's cheaper to manufacture. ❹ It still achieves CPO-level power savings. The 2026-2027 transition hero, buying time until CPO matures. 完美的折衷方案。傳統可插拔模組像是站在大門口的翻譯員；CPO則把翻譯員焊進老闆的大腦裡。NPO取中間路線：把翻譯員搬到緊貼老闆辦公桌旁（距晶片僅毫米），但是插槽式，沒有焊死。這一舉解決了CPO的四大問題：❶壞了直接拔出——不需報廢整張主板。❷避開了晶片旁的散熱地獄。❸製造成本更低。❹仍然達到CPO級別的省電效果。2026-2027年的過渡期主角，為CPO成熟爭取時間。
AVGOEoptolinkLightspeedLightmatter

CPO Co-Packaged Optics共封裝光學

The ultimate weapon — weld the translator directly into the boss's brain. The optical engine is co-packaged with the compute chip itself, achieving an unbeatable 2-3 pJ/bit (vs 6-14 for pluggable). But four problems remain unsolved: ❶ Repair: Imagine your lightbulb is welded to the house's foundation — one dead laser means scrapping a board worth tens of thousands of dollars (64+ ports down). ❷ Environment: Perfect in cleanrooms, but real datacenters have dust, vibration, and heat that degrade precision alignment. ❸ Cost: Do electricity savings really offset the massive upfront CapEx? ❹ Supply chain: External laser source and fiber routing specs are still being written. NVIDIA is shipping CPO switches for Scale-out now, but Scale-up CPO won't arrive until 2028. 終極武器——把翻譯官直接焊進老闆的大腦裡。光學引擎與運算晶片合封，達到無敵的2-3 pJ/bit（可插拔為6-14）。但四大問題仍未解決：❶維修：想像你家客廳的燈泡跟整棟房子的地基焊死在一起——一顆雷射壞了，可能要報廢一張價值數萬美元的主板（64個以上端口停擺）。❷環境：無塵室裡完美，但真實機房有灰塵、震動和高溫，會破壞精密對準。❸成本：省下的電費真的能彌補前期高昂的建置成本嗎？❹供應鏈：外部雷射源和光纖走線規範仍在制定中。NVIDIA已在Scale-out出貨CPO交換器，但Scale-up的CPO要到2028年才會到來。
NVDAAVGOCOHRLITELightmatterAyar Labs

③ Copper's Last Stand — "The Highway Overpass"③ 銅線最後防線——「高架快速道路」

CPC Co-Packaged Copper共封裝銅纜

Engineers asked: "If the distance is under 1.5 meters, why bother converting electrons to photons and back?" CPC's answer: skip the motherboard entirely. It pulls special high-speed copper cables (Twinax) directly from the chip substrate, like building a highway overpass that flies over city streets. The signal avoids the lossy PCB "surface roads" below. This is copper's last fortress — zero optical conversion, decades-mature ecosystem, cheapest option. But the physics ceiling arrives sooner than optical: at PCIe Gen6+ / 200G+/lane, effective copper reach shrinks below 1 meter. Marvell itself admits CPC is "paving the road for the optical transition." 工程師們問：「距離不到1.5公尺，為什麼要費事把電子轉成光子再轉回來？」CPC的答案：完全跳過主機板。它直接從晶片基板拉出特製高速銅纜（Twinax），像建造一條飛越城市街道的高架快速道路，訊號避開了下方損耗嚴重的PCB「地面道路」。這是銅線的最後堡壘——零光電轉換，數十年成熟生態系，成本最低。但物理極限比光學更早到來：PCIe Gen6+/200G+/lane時，銅線有效傳輸距離縮至1公尺以下。Marvell自己都承認CPC是「為光學過渡鋪路」。
SamtecTELMRVLALAB

AEC Active Electrical Cables主動電纜

While CPC handles connections inside the box (chip-to-chip, <1.5m), AEC handles connections between boxes (switch-to-switch, 2-7m). These are copper cables with built-in retimer chips that regenerate the signal mid-flight. Credo is the dominant market leader, already adopted by AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Meta. The industry consensus: AEC is a "stop-gap" technology that generates real revenue right now during the copper-to-optical transition. Don't confuse CPC and AEC — they operate at different network layers and often peacefully coexist in the same rack. CPC處理盒子裡面的連接（晶片對晶片，<1.5m），而AEC處理盒子與盒子之間的連接（交換器對交換器，2-7m）。這些銅纜內建重定時器晶片，在傳輸途中再生訊號。Credo是市場領導者，已被AWS、微軟Azure和Meta採用。產業共識：AEC是在銅轉光過渡期中現在就產生真實營收的「過渡性」技術。不要混淆CPC和AEC——它們在不同的網路層級運作，經常在同一個機櫃裡和平共存。
CRDOMRVLALAB

④ OCS — "Tearing Down the Traffic Lights"④ OCS——「拆掉紅綠燈」

MEMS Micro-Mirror OCS (Lumentum)微機電鏡面OCS（Lumentum）

In traditional datacenters, light signals must stop at electronic "traffic lights" (switches), convert to electricity, queue, process, then convert back to light. Google said: tear down the traffic lights and build an optical crossroads. Their Apollo system uses thousands of microscopic MEMS mirrors that physically tilt to redirect light beams — pure reflection, no electrical conversion. Result: 95% less power (108W max vs thousands for electronic switches), 30% more throughput, 30% less CapEx. And the most magical property: speed-immune. A mirror doesn't care if it reflects 40G or 800G light — the same infrastructure works across generations. Lumentum builds these, with >$400M backlog and a $1B+ OCS revenue target by 2027. The trade-off: MEMS needs 100-200V drive and has mechanical fatigue risk over decades. 在傳統資料中心，光訊號必須在電子「紅綠燈」（交換器）前停下，轉成電訊號，排隊、處理，再轉回光。Google說：把紅綠燈拆了，直接蓋一座光學十字路口。他們的Apollo系統使用數千面微小的MEMS鏡面物理性傾斜來重定向光束——純反射，無電氣轉換。結果：功耗降95%（最大108W vs 電子交換器數千瓦），吞吐量提升30%，CapEx節省30%。最神奇的特性：對速率免疫。鏡面不在乎反射的是40G還是800G的光——同一套基礎設施跨世代使用。Lumentum製造這些設備，積壓訂單>$4億，目標2027年OCS營收$10億+。代價：MEMS需要100-200V高電壓驅動，數十年後有機械疲勞風險。
LITEMRVL

LCoS Liquid Crystal OCS (Coherent)液晶OCS（Coherent）

Coherent took a completely different path. Instead of tiny tilting mirrors, their OCS works like a liquid crystal TV — light passes through a layer of liquid crystal whose molecular alignment is electrically changed to refract and redirect the beam. The key advantage: zero moving parts. Drive voltage is under 10V (like a remote control battery), compared to MEMS' 100-200V. Coherent claims: lower failure rate, longer lifespan. The trade-off: less production track record than MEMS, which has been running in Google's datacenters for years. This "tilting mirrors vs solid-state liquid crystal" battle is one of the most fascinating technology rivalries in the entire ecosystem. Coherent走了一條完全不同的路。不用微小的傾斜鏡面，他們的OCS像一台液晶電視——光穿過一層液晶材料，系統電氣改變液晶分子的排列方式，讓光線折射並重定向。關鍵優勢：完全沒有運動零件。驅動電壓低於10V（像遙控器電池），而MEMS需要100-200V。Coherent宣稱：更低故障率、更長壽命。代價：量產實績不如MEMS，MEMS已在Google資料中心運行多年。這場「傾斜鏡面 vs 固態液晶」之戰，是整個生態系中最迷人的技術對決之一。
COHR

⑤ Silicon Photonics & Materials — "The Cost Revolution"⑤ 矽光子與材料——「成本革命」

SiPh Silicon Photonics矽光子

Silicon is the world's best calculator, but it has one fatal flaw: it cannot emit light. Silicon Photonics takes advantage of existing TSMC/Intel/GlobalFoundries CMOS fabrication lines to print optical circuits at massive scale and ultra-low cost — the same factories that make your phone's chips. But for the "light source" it still needs an InP (Indium Phosphide) laser chip bonded on top. This is driving heterogeneous integration: SiPh backbone + TFLN modulator + InP laser, assembled by foundries using advanced packaging (CoWoS-R, Foveros). Long-term, this shifts power from traditional optical companies toward semiconductor foundries becoming optical "assembly plants." 矽是世界上最好的計算器，但有一個致命缺陷：它不會發光。矽光子利用現有的台積電/Intel/GlobalFoundries CMOS生產線，以極低成本大規模印製光學線路——跟製造你手機晶片的同一批工廠。但「光源」仍然需要一顆InP（磷化銦）雷射晶片鍵合在上面。這驅動了異質整合：SiPh骨幹 + TFLN調變器 + InP雷射，由晶圓代工廠使用先進封裝（CoWoS-R/Foveros）組裝。長期來看，這將權力從傳統光學公司轉移到半導體代工廠成為光學「總裝廠」。
InnolightTSMCIntelGFSLightmatterAyar Labs

TFLN Thin Film Lithium Niobate薄膜鈮酸鋰 + EML

TFLN is a next-generation modulator material — like giving SiPh a "premium gearbox." At 1.6T+ speeds, silicon photonics modulators suffer from signal blurring (chirp) and increased loss. TFLN fixes this with ultra-low power, and critically, prevents the InP laser from overheating. Still faces brittleness and yield challenges in volume production.

EML (Electro-absorption Modulated Laser) is the universal "lightbulb + switch" combo chip made from InP. This is the core AI engine that every technology needs — whether CPO, LPO, XPO, or OCS, they all require an EML or similar InP laser to function. Lumentum holds 50-60% market share; Coherent is the only company mass-producing 6-inch InP wafers (4× the chip yield of standard 4-inch). The InP supply chain is the single highest-certainty chokepoint in the entire ecosystem. TFLN是下一代調變器材料——像給矽光子換上「頂級變速箱」。在1.6T+速度下，矽光子調變器會出現訊號模糊（啁啾效應）和損耗增加。TFLN以超低功耗修復這些問題，關鍵是防止InP雷射過熱。量產仍面臨脆性和良率挑戰。

EML（電吸收調變雷射）是由InP製成的通用「小燈泡+開關」合體晶片。這是每種技術都需要的核心AI引擎——不管是CPO、LPO、XPO還是OCS，都需要EML或類似的InP雷射才能運作。Lumentum掌握50-60%市佔率；Coherent是唯一量產6吋InP晶圓的公司（晶片良率為標準4吋的4倍）。InP供應鏈是整個生態系中確定性最高的咽喉。

UALink (Ultra Accelerator Link)

The anti-NVLink coalition. Open Scale-up interconnect standard challenging NVIDIA's proprietary NVLink. Founded by AMD, Broadcom, Cisco, Google, Intel, Meta, Microsoft. Critical for understanding the copper→optical transition dynamics — UALink defines how non-NVIDIA accelerators (Maia, MTIA, Trainium, TPU) will interconnect optically. 對抗NVLink聯盟。開放Scale-up互連標準，挑戰NVIDIA專有NVLink。由AMD、Broadcom、Cisco、Google、Intel、Meta、Microsoft發起。對理解銅→光轉換至關重要——UALink定義非NVIDIA加速器（Maia/MTIA/Trainium/TPU）如何進行光學互連。
